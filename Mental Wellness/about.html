About this Toolkit

This toolkit collects practical self-care techniques and educational resources to help you build daily routines, manage stress, and reach out for support when needed. It is not a substitute for professional care but aims to offer accessible strategies for everyday mental wellness.

Goals

  • Provide short, actionable self-care strategies
  • Help users form healthy routines
  • Offer resources for professional support
