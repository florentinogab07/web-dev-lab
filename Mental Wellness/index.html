About this toolkit

This toolkit adapts evidence-based, practical suggestions into short, actionable resources. It is intended to guide personal self-care and help you find professional support when needed. The content is informational and not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Contact & Support

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, call your local emergency number. For mental health resources, contact local health services or a licensed professional.
