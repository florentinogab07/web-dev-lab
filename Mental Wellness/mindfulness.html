Mindfulness & Journaling

Short practices that help focus and regulate mood:

  • 5-min breathing or body-scan meditation.
  • Gratitude journal: write 1–3 things you're grateful for daily.
  • Reflective prompts: "What helped me today?" "What do I need tomorrow?"
