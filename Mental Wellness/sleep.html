Sleep Hygiene Tips

  • Keep a consistent sleep/wake schedule.
  • Create a calm bedtime routine (no screens 30–60 min before bed).
  • Make your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet.
  • Avoid heavy meals, caffeine, and alcohol before bed.

If sleep problems persist, consider discussing with a healthcare provider.
