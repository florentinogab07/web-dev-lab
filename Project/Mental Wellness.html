3 Simple Breathing Exercises

  1. Box breathing: Inhale 4s → Hold 4s → Exhale 4s → Hold 4s. Repeat 4 cycles.
  2. 4-7-8 breath: Inhale 4s → Hold 7s → Exhale 8s. Repeat 4–6 cycles.
  3. Diaphragmatic breath: Breathe slowly into belly for calm — place hand on belly and expand, then exhale.

Try these seated or lying down. Stop if you feel lightheaded.

Quick Grounding

Use 5-4-3-2-1: name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.

Sleep Hygiene Tips

  • Keep a consistent sleep/wake schedule.
  • Create a calm bedtime routine (no screens 30–60 min before bed).
  • Make your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet.
  • Avoid heavy meals, caffeine, and alcohol before bed.

If sleep problems persist, consider discussing with a healthcare provider.

Nutrition & Hydration

Healthy eating supports mood and energy. Try these simple habits:

  • Drink water regularly (start day with a glass).
  • Include protein, healthy fats, and fiber at meals.
  • Snack on fruit, nuts, or yogurt instead of high-sugar treats.

Movement & Short Routines

Movement helps mood — try these short options:

  • 5–10 minute stretch break every hour during long sitting sessions.
  • 20–30 minute brisk walk daily.
  • Simple bodyweight circuit: squats, push-ups, lunges (2 rounds).

Mindfulness & Journaling

Short practices that help focus and regulate mood:

  • 5-min breathing or body-scan meditation.
  • Gratitude journal: write 1–3 things you're grateful for daily.
  • Reflective prompts: "What helped me today?" "What do I need tomorrow?"
