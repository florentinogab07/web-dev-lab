Tips
Social anxiety, Panic attacks, Depression
Solitude makes it hard to seek help or support when needed.
Try gentle group activities or therapy to connect with others.
Depression, Burnout
High standards often feel impossible to meet.
Learn to accept progress, not perfection.
Anxiety, Panic Attacks
Constantly imagining worst-case scenarios drains energy.
Practice mindfulness to calm catastrophic thoughts.
Bipolar disorder, Depression
Creative intensity may cause imbalance in focus.
Balance imagination with grounding routines.
Anxiety
Emotional highs and lows lead to stress and tension.
Reflect on triggers, practice grounding exercises.
Anxiety, Depression, Eating disorders
Self-esteem depends on constant achievement.
Celebrate small wins and practice self-kindness.
